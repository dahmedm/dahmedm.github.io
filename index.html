About Me

I am a PhD student in Biological and Environmental Engineering at Cornell University, working under the supervision of Prof. Manoj Karkee in the AgRobotics Lab. My research focuses on developing autonomous ground robots for fruit orchard environments, with emphasis on navigation systems, computer vision, and automated pruning operations.

I have published multiple peer-reviewed papers in leading agricultural engineering journals and have presented my work at international conferences. My expertise spans machine learning, computer vision, robotics, and precision agriculture.
